About Us

We're a team of creative thinkers, problem solvers, and digital craftspeople passionate about building meaningful experiences.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Intine emerged from a simple belief: great design should be invisible. We started as a small team of designers and developers who were frustrated with the status quo of digital experiences. Too often, we saw beautiful designs that failed to serve their purpose, or functional products that lacked soul.

Today, we're a growing team of strategists, designers, developers, and marketers who work together to create experiences that are both beautiful and purposeful. We believe that the best design solutions come from deep understanding of users, business goals, and technical constraints.

From initial research to final implementation, we work closely with your team to ensure every decision aligns with your vision and business objectives. We believe that great design is the result of great collaboration.
